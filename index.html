David Balbás

I am a first-year PhD student in Cryptography at IMDEA Software Institute in Madrid. I obtained a MSc in Computer Science (specializing in theoretical computer science) at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm.

I did my MSc Thesis at LASEC, EPFL in Lausanne. Previously, I completed a BSc in Mathematics and a BSc in Physics at Universidad de Cantabria, Spain.

Interests

I am broadly interested in cryptography, computer security, complexity theory and mathematics. Currently, my work focuses on verifiable computation (mostly zk-SNARKs and their applications), and on group messaging.
