David Balbás

I am a first-year PhD student in Cryptography at IMDEA Software Institute in Madrid, advised by Prof. Dario Fiore and Prof. Maribel González-Vasco. I obtained a MSc in Computer Science (with a focus on theoretical computer science) at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm.

I did my MSc Thesis at LASEC, EPFL in Lausanne, under the supervision of Prof. Serge Vaudenay and Daniel Collins. Previously, I completed a BSc in Mathematics and a BSc in Physics at Universidad de Cantabria, Spain.

Interests

My interests include cryptography, computer security, complexity theory and mathematics. Particularly, I am interested in group messaging, lattice-based cryptography, and verifiable computation.
